Who We Are


We believe that life must be celebrated, especially those events that represent milestones in our journey. Your birthday, graduation, wedding day, anniversary, work promotion, your child's birthday, the success of your business or organisation; these are just some of the special occasions we should be grateful for. Our goal is to help you celebrate these milestones.


We provide a comprehensive range of equipment for hosting events including tents, tables, and chairs. We also cater for all types of events including weddings, parties, conferences and corporate functions.
